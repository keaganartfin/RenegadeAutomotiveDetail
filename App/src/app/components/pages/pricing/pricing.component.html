Ceramic Coatings

The Power of Ceramic Coatings

The most recent development in paint protection are products commonly referred to as glass or ceramic coatings. These coatings are made from materials that are more durable than the polymers found in traditional sealants and waxes giving you a more durable longer-lasting protection.

In addition to forming a very strong bond with the clear coat finish, a coating is generally much thicker than a wax or sealant, creating a sacrificial layer that can absorb damage and better protect the paint underneath. The thickness gives it a great depth of gloss that’s hard to achieve with other products. Most coatings will easily give years of protection when properly applied.

At Renegade Automotive we use Gtechniq's line of Ceramic Coatings. This plus a complete two-step paint correction and decontamination is the formula to making your vehicle look brand new for longer and maintain a high level of protection for all scenarios.
